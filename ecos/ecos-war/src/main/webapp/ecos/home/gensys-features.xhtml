Depending on the unique design, content and configuration of the target system, various capabilities will be enabled. Some of these are:

Service Invocation - Several ways of invoking services are supported: Web-services, REST-services, JMS MDB, EJB, RMI, as well as CORBA.

Enterprise Messaging - based constructs are generated to implement specific . Well established are generated when needed.

Distributed Caching – Configuration is the key. Defaults are provided and may be customized at generation time, and at runtime.

Data Persistence – JPA based data layer implementation is generated along with SQL schema. Custom relationships and queries may be added in the model.

Transaction Support – Generated service and process components will execute within short-term as well as long-term transactions.

Event Propagation – Application event logging, persistence, subscription and monitoring is supported.
