Depending on the unique design, content and configuration of the target system, various capabilities will be enabled. Some of these are: – Several ways of invoking services are supported: Web–services, REST–services, JMS MDB, EJB, RMI, as well as CORBA. – JMS based constructs are generated to implement specific Messaging Architectures. – Configuration is the key. Defaults are provided and may be customized at generation time, and at runtime. – JPA based data layer implementation is generated along with SQL schema. Custom relationships and queries may be added in the model. – Generated service and process components will execute within short-term as well as long-term transactions. — — persistence, subscription and monitoring is supported —
